TWICE lyric successions in their Korean singles

The visualization shows how many times a TWICE member lyric succeeeds another member in their korean singles. Order is preserved (e.g. a Jihyo-Nayeon succession is different from a Nayeon-Jihyo succession). The thicker a line, the more often the succession pair happened.

Data and analysis by JC Peralta. Base viz code provided by Nadieh Bremer
The songs considered for this analysis are: Cheer Up, Cry For Me, Dance The Night Away, Fancy, Feel Special, I Can't Stop Me, Heart Shaker, Knock Knock, LikeOohAhh, Likey, More&More, Signal, TT, What Is Love, Yes Or Yes,
